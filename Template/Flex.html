About OneDeal

This is a prototype of an e-commerce automobile platform created as a part of a self learning project to implement multiple concepts such as Data Science, Machine Learning, SQL, Flask and Web Design. Using this application, you can -

Search for Cars

Don't know where to search for your dream car ? No Problem! Using our database of more than 6000 vehicles listed for sales, search for your dream cars from the best brands available, at the location of your preference and most importantly, within your budget.

List Cars for Sale

Want to sell you car but don't know where to list it? No Problem! Using the SQLite and Flaskforms, we have created a interface which facilitates storing your car's information in a database of car sellers. List your car on OneDeal and get connected to a large pool of buyers interested in your car.

Get the Best Price

One of the biggest problems for anyone selling their car. Want to put up your car for sale but don't know how to decide it's price ? No Problem! Use the Best Price Predictor, based on a Machine Learning algorithm, get rid of your headache to decide price and let us give you the price within seconds.
